# How to use your PA System (this lives in YOUR folder as HOW-TO-USE.md — the one-glance card)

## The one habit that matters
**Just talk.** Your **Chief of Staff** is always the default — it figures out which specialist
your request needs and brings them in itself. You never have to pick.

## Your team (they come to you — but you CAN call them by name)
| Say | Who steps in | For |
|-----|--------------|-----|
| *(nothing — just ask)* | **Chief of Staff** | routes everything, holds your goals |
| "PA, …" | **PA** | errands, shopping, research, admin, follow-ups |
| "Venture, …" | **Venture** | business/product ideas, pressure-testing |
| "Build, …" | **Build** | apps, sites, mockups, data |
| "X, …" | **X** | posts, content, audience |
| "Relations, …" | **Relations** | reaching out, staying in touch (drafts only) |
| "Architect, …" | **Architect** | improving this system, adding specialists |
| "Red Team this" | **Red Team** | stress-testing a plan |
| "bring in a ‹domain› expert" | **Expert Bench** | knowledge nobody standing has |

Their full playbooks live in your `modes/` folder — yours to read, never required.

## Starting a session
- **Inside your "Everyday PA System" Project (recommended):** open a new **Cowork** chat and just say
  **hello** — it knows you automatically. Seems not to? Reconnect the folder (HELP.md §1).
- **Outside the Project (a plain chat):** connect your folder to the chat, then paste the
  **connect prompt** from `HELP.md` — the same block that lives in your Project's Instructions —
  every time. (Or add the Global Instruction once — see HELP.)
- Use **Claude Cowork** for anything that needs your computer or files.

## Ending a session — say "wrap up"
Before you leave, say **"wrap up."** Your session is saved to `memory/log/` — decisions, open
items, where to pick up. **Don't just close the laptop** — unsaved sessions are forgotten ones.

## The rules your team never breaks
Never sends, buys, or connects anything without your ok on that specific item. Never marks work
"Done" — everything comes back **"ready for your ok."** Your memory is plain files in this
folder — open them any time; if something's wrong, say so and it gets fixed.

## Growing it
"PA, help me with travel too" (a new domain) · "start a task: ‹thing›" · "Architect, add a
specialist for ‹X›" · "deepen my vault" (it learns you deeper). Everything grows additively —
nothing you've built gets overwritten.

*Something broken or confusing? → `HELP.md`, same folder.*
