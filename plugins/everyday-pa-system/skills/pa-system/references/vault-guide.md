# Vault Guide — deepen my vault (optional; copied to the user's folder as memory/context/VAULT-GUIDE.md)

_Your PA works fine with the short profile from setup. This guide is for whenever you want it to
know you deeper — say **"deepen my vault"** and it runs ONE area below per session (your pick),
bounded like everything else: a few questions, then it writes what it learned and stops._

## The areas (pick one at a time; docs beat questions — share anything that already exists)
1. **Values under pressure** — what you'd fight for even when inconvenient; what you judge in others;
   where your stated values and actual behavior diverge. → sharpens every judgment call made for you.
2. **Energy map** — what gives you energy; what drains you *even when you're good at it*.
   → tells your PA exactly what to take off your plate and what to protect.
3. **Reality & constraints** — career, situation, the real limits (time, money, health, place).
   → keeps advice grounded in your actual life, not a fantasy version.
4. **Your people** — family, friends, collaborators worth a file. → context for scheduling, gifts,
   outreach, and who matters when.
5. **Underused leverage** — strengths, assets, relationships you're sitting on. → where cheap wins hide.
6. **Who you're becoming** — the longer arc beyond this year's goal. → lets the system aim ahead of you.
7. **Your bar** — what "genuinely great work" looks like to you. → calibrates every deliverable.

## Rules (same as everywhere)
- One area per session · a handful of questions max · write to the Vault immediately · show what was
  written · truth over aspiration · the learning loop keeps deepening these through real work anyway.
