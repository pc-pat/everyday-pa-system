# The Everyday PA System (built on Claude Cowork) — v0.8.1

A team of specialist assistants that **knows you** and runs your **life and work**, end to end —
its memory lives in **your own files, on your computer**, not in a chat. The kind of support
that used to be reserved for the wealthy, in everyone's hands.

## Install & begin (~10 minutes to your first win) — full steps in INSTALL.md
1. Install this plugin (Settings → Plugins → install the `.plugin` file). The plugin page may
   list a skill as "/pa-system" — ignore it; **plain phrases are the interface.**
2. Open a **Claude Cowork** session — **not a plain chat** (setup needs to create your folder;
   check the Chat/Cowork switch).
3. Say: **"Set up my Everyday PA System"** — those exact words. The wizard does
   everything with you: creates your folder (and proves it works), learns who you are — at most
   a handful of questions — then lands your first real win.

Interrupted mid-setup? New Cowork chat → say **"Set up my Everyday PA System"** again — it
resumes exactly where you stopped. **Daily use after setup: open a Cowork chat inside your
"Everyday PA System" Project and just say hello.**

## Your team — you never pick, it routes itself
Your **Chief of Staff** is the default: just talk, and it brings in the right specialist —
PA (life & admin) · Venture (ideas → products) · Build (apps & sites) · X (audience) ·
Relations (people) · Architect (the system itself) — plus a Red Team that stress-tests plans
and an Expert Bench for anything else. All nine playbooks are installed into your own
`modes/` folder: a real team on disk, yours.

## Four things to know
1. **Your memory is yours** — plain files in your own folder. Open and read them any time.
2. **Your team lives inside your "Everyday PA System" Project** — a new Cowork chat there +
   "hello" is all it takes. Outside the Project, a plain chat needs your folder connected AND
   the connect prompt from HELP.md — the same block that's in your Project's Instructions (or
   the Global Instruction the wizard offers).
3. **Say "wrap up" before you leave** — that's how sessions are saved. Don't just close the
   laptop.
4. **It never acts without you** — never sends, never buys, never connects anything without
   your ok. Work comes back **"ready for your ok,"** never silently "done."
