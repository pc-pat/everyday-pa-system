# The Everyday PA System

**Your personal AI team that turns intent into done.**

Not a chatbot and not another app to learn — a *team* of specialist AI assistants that knows
you, holds your context, and runs your life and work end to end. One mind, one memory, many
modes. Built on Claude Cowork.



---

## What you need

- A paid Claude plan (**Pro, Max, Team, or Enterprise**) — plugins aren't available on the free plan.
- The **Claude desktop app** with **Cowork** (setup creates a folder on your computer and shows its progress).

## Install

1. In the Claude app, open **Customize** (left sidebar) → **Plugins**.
2. In **Personal plugins**, click **+ → Add marketplace → Add from a repository**.
3. Paste this repository's URL and confirm.
4. Find **The Everyday PA System** in the list and click **Install**.
5. **Relaunch the app** when prompted so the skill registers.

When a new version ships, you'll get it automatically — just **relaunch** to update.

## Start it

Open a new **Cowork** session (not a plain chat) and type:

> **Set up my Everyday PA System**

A guided wizard does the rest in about ten minutes — it creates your folder, gets to know you,
and lands a first real win. You answer, approve, and confirm; it does the thinking.

*(Tip: use the phrase above — not a slash command.)*

## What it does

Once set up, you have a Chief of Staff that routes every request to the right specialist:

- **PA** — travel, bills, bookings, inbox, errands.
- **Venture** — shaping ideas and products.
- **Build** — apps, pages, tools.
- **X** — posts and public presence.
- **Relations** — outreach and people.
- **Architect** — improving the system itself.

…plus a **Red Team** to stress-test a plan and an **Expert Bench** for any domain. They all share
one memory that lives in your folder, so the system remembers you and gets better over time.

## Your control, always

It never sends a message, makes a payment, handles a password or 2FA, installs anything, or takes
any irreversible action without your explicit go on that specific thing — and it never marks work
"done," only "pending your confirmation."

## Trust & privacy

This plugin is **skills-only** — it does not run a background server on your machine. Your memory
lives in plain files in a folder you own, on your computer.
